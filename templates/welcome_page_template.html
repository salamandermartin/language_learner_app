Welcome to Phase Uno's Language Learning Application!

This application is created by Martin Salamanca, Hans Vidaure, and Orzeeta Kamal. The purpose of this app is to make learning new languages more fun. It is designed to translate a list of words the user entered into their desired language of translation, both in singular and plural forms.
